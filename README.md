I'm a **PhD Student in Computer Science** at Sapienza University of Rome, currently visiting **ETH Zurich**. My research focuses on **user behavior in distributed systems**, including blockchains and social networks.

I'm currently building [**BigBrotr**](https://bigbrotr.com), an open-source observability infrastructure for the **Nostr protocol**, funded by an **OpenSats grant**. It archives events, monitors relays, and enables network-wide analysis.

I also enjoy participating in **hackathons**, building blockchain protocols, smart contracts, and cryptographic systems.
